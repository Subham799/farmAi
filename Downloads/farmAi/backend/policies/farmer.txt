*Government Schemes for Farmers
Central Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN): Provides ₹6,000 annually to eligible farmers in three equal installments.  Beneficiaries can now update Aadhaar and KYC online.    
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY): Offers crop insurance against natural calamities, pests, and diseases.    
Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY): Aims to expand cultivable area under assured irrigation.    
Soil Health Card Scheme: Provides soil health cards to farmers with crop-wise recommendations.    
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY): Promotes organic farming through a cluster-based approach.    
National Agriculture Market (e-NAM): A pan-India online trading platform for agricultural commodities.  Farmers get better prices and transparency in trade.    
Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme: Provides timely credit support to farmers for their cultivation needs.    
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana (PM-KMY): Pension scheme for small and marginal farmers.    
Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY): Provides financial assistance to states for agriculture development.    
National Food Security Mission (NFSM): Aims to increase production of rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals, and commercial crops.    
National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP): Enhances oilseeds production and productivity.    
National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA): Promotes sustainable agriculture practices.    
National Horticulture Mission (NHM): Promotes holistic growth of the horticulture sector.    
National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET): Strengthens agricultural extension services.    
National Mission on Micro Irrigation (NMMI): Promotes efficient irrigation practices.    
National Bamboo Mission (NBM): Promotes holistic development of the bamboo sector.    
National Mission on Medicinal Plants: Promotes cultivation of medicinal plants.    
National Agroforestry Policy: Encourages tree plantation on farmland.    
Agri-Clinics and Agri-Business Centres Scheme: Supports agricultural graduates to establish agri-ventures.    
Integrated Scheme for Agricultural Marketing (ISAM): Develops marketing infrastructure.    
Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM): Promotes farm mechanization.    
Sub-Mission on Plant Protection and Plant Quarantine (SMPPQ): Ensures plant health management.    
Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Material (SMSP): Ensures availability of quality seeds.    
Sub-Mission on Agricultural Extension (SMAE): Strengthens extension services.    
Sub-Mission on Agricultural Statistics (SMAS): Improves the agricultural statistics system.    
Sub-Mission on Agriculture Education (SMAE): Enhances agricultural education.    
Sub-Mission on Agriculture Research and Education (SMARE): Promotes agricultural research and education.    
National Programme for Organic Production (NPOP): Develops organic agriculture.    
National Programme on Use of Space Technology for Agriculture: Utilizes space technology for agricultural development.    
National e-Governance Plan in Agriculture (NeGP-A): Promotes IT in agriculture.    
Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH): Integrates horticulture development.    
National Livestock Mission (NLM): Enhances livestock productivity.    
Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS): Promotes dairy farming.    
Rashtriya Gokul Mission: Conserves and develops indigenous bovine breeds.    
National Programme for Bovine Breeding and Dairy Development: Improves bovine productivity.    
National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) Schemes: Promotes the fisheries sector.    
Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries: Enhances fish production.    
Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY): Develops the fisheries sector.  Accident Insurance Scheme is part of this.    
National Mission on Edible Oils – Oil Palm (NMEO-OP): Promotes oil palm cultivation.  Targets self-sufficiency in edible oils.    
National Beekeeping and Honey Mission (NBHM): Promotes beekeeping and honey production.    
National Mission on Food Processing (NMFP): Develops the food processing sector.    
Mega Food Parks Scheme: Establishes food processing units.    
Agri-Stack Digital Platform (Under Development): Each farmer will have a unique digital ID linked with land, crops, and financials.  Will help with personalized subsidies, loans, and insurance.    
FPOs (Farmer Producer Organizations) Scheme: Supports the formation of registered farmer groups for collective marketing and input purchase.  ₹15 lakh grant for registered FPOs.    
PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PM-FME): Provides help for food-processing units and farm-level storage solutions.  Includes credit-linked subsidy and training.    
Kisan Drone Scheme: Provides subsidies for drones to be used for fertilizer/pesticide spraying and crop monitoring and soil data.  50-100% subsidy for certain groups (SC/ST, FPOs).    
Solar Pump Yojana (PM-KUSUM): Offers up to 60% subsidy for solar-powered pumps.  Reduces dependency on diesel/electricity.    
Atmanirbhar Bharat Agriculture Infrastructure Fund: Provides medium-long term loans at 3% interest subvention.  Aims to build godowns, cold storage, and supply chains.    
Livestock Health & Insurance Scheme: Covers healthcare and insurance for livestock, especially for dairy farmers and goat rearing.

•	A pan-India online trading platform for agri-produce.
•	Farmers get better prices and transparency in trade.
3. Agri-Stack Digital Platform (Under Development)
•	Each farmer will have a unique digital ID linked with land, crops, and financials.
•	Will help with personalized subsidies, loans, and insurance.
4. FPOs (Farmer Producer Organizations) Scheme
•	Support to form registered farmer groups for collective marketing and input purchase.
•	₹15 lakh grant for registered FPOs.
5. PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PM-FME)
•	Help for food-processing units and farm-level storage solutions.
•	Credit-linked subsidy + training.
6. Kisan Drone Scheme
•	Subsidies for drones to be used for:
o	Fertilizer/pesticide spraying
o	Crop monitoring and soil data
•	50-100% subsidy for certain groups (SC/ST, FPOs)
7. Solar Pump Yojana (PM-KUSUM)
•	Up to 60% subsidy for solar-powered pumps.
•	Reduces dependency on diesel/electricity.
8. Atmanirbhar Bharat Agriculture Infrastructure Fund
•	Medium-long term loans at 3% interest subvention.
•	Build godowns, cold storage, and supply chains.
9. Livestock Health & Insurance Scheme
•	Covers health care and insurance for livestock.
•	Especially for dairy farmers and goat rearing.
10. National Mission on Edible Oils – Oil Palm (NMEO-OP)
•	Financial support for oil palm cultivation & processing.
•	Targets self-sufficiency in edible oils.
